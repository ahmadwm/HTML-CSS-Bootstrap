Woej is a professional organization for Social Work in Leidschendam-Voorburg. Knowing each other, helping each other is our mission statement. Together with residents, network partners and the municipality, we are building a pleasant, safe, involved society in which everyone participates. We know what is going on in the living environment. We are close and accessible. We actively promote citizenship, initiate and strengthen social networks, support neighborhood initiatives and we connect residents and networks. Woej supports people with self-reliance and social participation, from their own strength and with the efforts of the environment. If self-reliance is lacking, we offer adequate help or engage it.
